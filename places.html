PLACES TO VISIT

Connaught Place

Connaught Place is famous for its rich colonial architecture, which houses the various shops and restaurants of the city.

Lotus Temple

Notable for its flowerlike shape, it has become a prominent attraction in the city.

Raj Ghat

Built in the memory of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, who is revered as the Father of the Nation

Akshardham Temple

Swaminarayan Akshardham is a Hindu temple, and spiritual-cultural campus in Delhi, India.

Sarojini market

Sarojini Nagar Market is famous mainly because of inexpensive and very low price clothes on sale which has good quality
